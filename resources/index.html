    Recommended Parts

      You will need an Elite-C, LEDs (optional), plate-mount Cherry-style stabilizers, switches, and keycaps to complete your build. I recommend Durock Plate Mount Stabilizers from 1upkeyboards and 1.8 mm LEDs from 1upkeyboards. Conveniently, 1upkeyboards also stock the Elite-C and various switches and keycaps.
